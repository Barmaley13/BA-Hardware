Files included:

PCB Fab Requirements:
	- 2-Layer PCB Stackup
	- 1oz Top and Bottom Copper Thickness
	- Finish: ENIG
	- Soldermask on top and bottom
	- Silkscreen on top and bottom
	- PCB Thickness: 0.062"
	- PCB Material: FR-04

	stepper_driver.bcream.ger		Bottom Paste
	stepper_driver.bottomsilkscreeen.ger	Bottom Silkscreen
	stepper_driver.bottomsoldermask.ger	Bottom Soldermask
